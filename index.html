About the Game

The best presidential election simulation, geared towards fun 1 or 2 player games that will have you at the edge of your seat when the votes come in on Election Day!

Do you have what it takes to become President of the United States?

Run for President is a fun, fast-paced election simulator that blends strategy, civics, and gameplay into an experience kids and adults can enjoy together. Play head-to-head like a board game using a single iPad/iPhone, or use it as an engaging classroom learning tool.

NO IN APP PURCHASES EVER! You get the entire game - enjoy!

Multiple Ways to Play

  • Simulation Mode: Replay real election matchups and test your campaign strategy
  • Fantasy Mode: Unlock all scenarios and create dream matchups across eras
  • 2-Player Board Game Style: One iPad or iPhone supports head-to-head play
  • Built for Learning + Fun
