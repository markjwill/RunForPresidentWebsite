About the Game

Election: Run For President is an engaging educational game that teaches players about the United States Electoral College system through strategic gameplay.

Navigate the complex landscape of American presidential elections as you campaign across states, manage resources, and make strategic decisions to win the Electoral College. Learn how presidential campaigns work while having fun!
